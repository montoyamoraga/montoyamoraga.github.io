design of electronic interfaces

abstract

in March 2022 i joined the faculty of University of Chile, Chile's premier state university, where i teach both requisite and elective courses in the undergraduate school of design.

"design of electronic interfaces", is an Arduino-based elective course that i created, wrote and taught my first semester at the University to more than 30 students with no programming or electronics experience.

because of the success of this course, the next semester i was promoted to teach two further undergraduate courses: the compulsory "physics applied to designers", and another elective i created, "design of digital musical instruments", for a total of 120 students this year.

class notes and code are published under an MIT license at https://github.com/montoyamoraga/aud5i022-2022-1
