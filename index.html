hi! my name is aarón montoya-moraga, i was born in chile in 1989

i go by the pronouns they/them, in case you wan to contact me

in 2019 i joined the poetic justice research group at mit media lab as a graduate student and research assistant

before that, i got my master's at new york university's interactive telecommunications program (2015-2017), and then stayed as a research resident (2017-2018)

in 2016 i founded and run the record label bandurria. where i showcase latin american new media art

in 2016 i co-founded coded escuela, a media arts school based in santiago de chile, focused on teaching teach introduction to programming for arts using open-source software, currently on hiatus

i volunteer for the processing foundation & the open-source project p5.js, & released both their website & introduction to programming book in spanish

i also contribute to the projects kinectron & new latin wave festival
