soldering studies

abstract

following graduation from MIT Media Lab, i returned to Chile and have refined my skills in soldering through the building of DIY musical instruments. i source the instruments from various companies that i am researching; below is a selection of completed projects.

  • ordering custom PCBs from manufacturers JLCPCB and OSHpark
  • founding and incorporating my own small business an for the manufacture and research of musical instruments and art projects in Chile

Ciat-Lonbarde Gerassic Organ

the Gerassic Organ is an analog synth, with tuning potentiometer, and different options for resistors and capacitors for alternative base tunings, and attack and decay times. it was created by Ciat-Lonbarde / Peter Blasser, and the PCB design is by crücFX.

i have created several iterations of this instrument, and released it as the first product of piruetas, the small business i founded in 2022 for distributing my artistic output. here is a paying customer at a local art fair, who acquired their synth for 30 USD, including taxes.

Oskitone APC

APC stands for Atari Punk Console, an analog synthesizer first described by Forrest M. Mims III, featuring a timer integrated circuit.

Oskitone Scout

the Scout is a digital ATMEGA-328P powered synth, which encourages hacking via reprogramming its firmware using the Arduino IDE.

Oskitone Poly 555

the Poly555 is an analog synth celebrating the 555 timer IC. each of the 20 keys has its own circuit, on/off LED, and tuning potentiometer.

BASTL microGranny2

the microGranny2 is one of the early projects by Czech company BASTL. it is an open source software and hardware mono granular sampler, with an embedded electret microphone.
