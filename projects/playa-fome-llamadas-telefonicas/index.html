Playa Fome - Llamadas Telefónicas

abstract

between 2018 and 2019 i recorded, mixed and co-produced Chilean artist Playa Fome's first EP, called Llamadas Telefónicas. since then i have collaborated with Playa Fome in further mixing and publishing of the artist's output, including Ssshh, his second EP. these releases and other music projects are done under my record label bandurria.

recording and mixing process

the EP was recorded and mixed at the bandurria recording studio. here i house my instrument collection, that i play and research as a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and instrument maker, including:

  • Critter And Guitari Pocket Piano synthesizer
  • Fender Bass VI baritone guitar
  • Moog Sustainer guitar
  • Suzuki Omnichord synthesizer and drum machine

all instruments, except a few exceptions were recorded by Playa Fome, with my assistance for MIDI and drum machine programming

further releases

Playa Fome's second EP was written and recorded during the pandemic, while he was based in France and i was in the USA, so i only did the mixing of this record, and we are currently working on his third EP
